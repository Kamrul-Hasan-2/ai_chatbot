# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is a chatbot?
A chatbot is an AI-powered software application that can simulate human conversation through text or voice interactions. Our chatbots use advanced natural language processing to understand and respond to user queries.

### How do I get started?
Getting started is easy:
1. Sign up for a free trial
2. Choose your plan
3. Set up your chatbot using our wizard
4. Deploy to your website or app
5. Start chatting!

### Do I need technical knowledge?
No! Our platform is designed for both technical and non-technical users. We provide:
- Easy-to-use visual builder
- Pre-built templates
- Step-by-step guides
- Video tutorials

## Features & Functionality

### What is RAG?
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is an advanced feature that allows the chatbot to retrieve relevant information from your knowledge base before responding, resulting in more accurate and contextual answers.

### Can the chatbot handle multiple conversations?
Yes! Our chatbots can handle unlimited simultaneous conversations (depending on your plan).

### Can I train the chatbot?
Yes! You can:
- Upload documents (PDF, Word, text)
- Add Q&A pairs
- Provide example conversations
- Fine-tune responses

### Does it support rich media?
Yes! Our chatbots can:
- Send images and GIFs
- Share videos
- Display buttons and quick replies
- Show carousels and lists
- Send files and documents
